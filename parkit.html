PARKit Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

PARKit is designed to help you park your thoughts and transition from work mode to rest mode. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data We Collect

PARKit does not require you to create an account.

PARKit does not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.

PARKit does not collect location data.

The keywords you enter are stored only on your device and are not sent to any server.

2. Analytics and Tracking

PARKit does not use advertising SDKs.

PARKit does not show ads.

PARKit does not use third-party tracking for marketing or advertising.

If in the future basic, privacy-respecting analytics are added, this policy will be updated before release of that version.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

PARKit offers an optional paid subscription to unlock additional features.

Purchases are processed securely through Google Play.

PARKit does not receive or store your payment information.

4. Notifications

If you enable notifications, PARKit stores your chosen times on your device to schedule reminders. These are not sent to any server.

5. Data Sharing

PARKit does not sell your data.

PARKit does not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or profiling.

Data may only be shared if required by law.

6. Data Security

Data stored by PARKit stays on your device and is protected by your device's own security.

If you uninstall the app, all data stored by PARKit is deleted.

7. Children and Minors

PARKit does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the policy changes significantly, a new version will be published at the same URL with an updated date.
