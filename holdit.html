HOLDit Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

HOLDit helps you hold and protect your real thoughts and moments. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data We Collect

HOLDit does not require you to create an account.

HOLDit does not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.

HOLDit does not collect location data.

All content you enter — moments, thoughts, titles, and notes — is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

2. Analytics and Tracking

HOLDit does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

HOLDit does not show ads.

HOLDit does not use third-party tracking for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

HOLDit offers an optional paid subscription to unlock additional features.

Purchases are processed securely through Google Play.

HOLDit does not receive or store your payment information.

4. Data Sharing

HOLDit does not sell your data.

HOLDit does not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or profiling.

Data may only be disclosed if required by law or to comply with a valid legal request.

5. Data Security

All data stored by HOLDit remains on your device and is protected by your device's own security, such as passcode or biometric lock.

If you uninstall the app, all data stored by HOLDit on your device is permanently deleted.

6. Children and Minors

HOLDit does not contain explicit or harmful content.

HOLDit does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a significant way, a new version will be published at the same URL with an updated date.
