TRACKit Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

TRACKit is a private app for holding a small number of serious ongoing tracks in life and reviewing how they develop over time. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data We Collect

TRACKit does not require you to create an account.

TRACKit does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other direct identifiers.

The information you enter into TRACKit — including track titles, context, timeline entries, dates, and related metadata — is stored only on your device.

In the current version, this data is not automatically synced to a remote server and is not shared with other users.

You may choose to enter personal or sensitive information in your tracks. This information remains under your control and is stored as app data on your device.

2. Analytics and Tracking

TRACKit does not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or advertising SDKs.

TRACKit does not show ads.

TRACKit does not use third-party tracking for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

TRACKit may offer optional paid features or subscriptions.

Purchases are processed through the platform’s store, such as Google Play or Apple’s App Store.

TRACKit does not store your full payment card details.

The store may provide limited transaction data, such as purchase status or product identifiers, so the app can determine whether paid features should be unlocked.

4. Data Sharing

TRACKit does not sell your data.

TRACKit does not share your personal content with third parties for advertising, profiling, or data-broker purposes.

Data may only be disclosed if required by law, legal process, or a valid governmental request, or when necessary to address fraud, security, or safety issues.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored by TRACKit remains on your device until you delete it from within the app or uninstall the app.

If you uninstall the app, the app’s local data on that device is deleted.

6. Data Security

All data stored by TRACKit remains on your device and is protected by your device’s own security, such as passcode or biometric lock.

The developer does not routinely access your content and there is no built-in mechanism in the current version to read your entries remotely.

7. Children and Minors

TRACKit is intended for general use and is not specifically targeted at children.

TRACKit does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

If the policy changes in a significant way, a new version will be published with an updated date.
