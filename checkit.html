CHECKit Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

CHECKit is a personal checklist app where you create named slots and store items to check off and revisit. The app is private, minimal, and free of accounts, social features, or external data collection.

1. Data We Collect

CHECKit does not require you to create an account.

CHECKit does not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.

CHECKit does not collect location data.

All content you enter — slot titles, checklist items, and timestamps — is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

2. Analytics and Tracking

CHECKit does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

CHECKit does not show ads.

CHECKit does not use third-party tracking for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

CHECKit offers an optional paid subscription to unlock additional slots.

Purchases are processed securely through Google Play.

CHECKit does not receive or store your payment information.

4. Data Sharing

CHECKit does not sell your data.

CHECKit does not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or profiling.

Data may only be disclosed if required by law or to comply with a valid legal request.

5. Data Security

All data stored by CHECKit remains on your device and is protected by your device's own security, such as passcode or biometric lock.

If you uninstall the app, all data stored by CHECKit on your device is permanently deleted.

6. Children and Minors

CHECKit does not contain explicit or harmful content.

CHECKit does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a significant way, a new version will be published at the same URL with an updated date.
