DIARYit Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

DIARYit is a private daily diary for naming your days. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data We Collect

DIARYit does not require you to create an account.

DIARYit does not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.

DIARYit does not collect location data.

All content you enter — day titles, dates, and labels — is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

2. Analytics and Tracking

DIARYit does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

DIARYit does not show ads.

DIARYit does not use third-party tracking for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

DIARYit offers an optional paid subscription to unlock full history and calendar features.

Purchases are processed securely through Google Play.

DIARYit does not receive or store your payment information.

4. Data Sharing

DIARYit does not sell your data.

DIARYit does not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or profiling.

Data may only be disclosed if required by law or to comply with a valid legal request.

5. Data Security

All data stored by DIARYit remains on your device and is protected by your device's own security, such as passcode or biometric lock.

If you uninstall the app, all data stored by DIARYit on your device is permanently deleted.

6. Children and Minors

DIARYit does not contain explicit or harmful content.

DIARYit does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a significant way, a new version will be published at the same URL with an updated date.
